    determinant of economic performance During the period  real GDP
    expanded at an annual average rate of about  Tourism makes a direct
    contribution to GDP of about  and also affects growth in other sectors 
    particularly in construction communications and public utilities Although
    Antigua and Barbuda is one of the few areas in the Caribbean experiencing a
    labor shortage in some sectors of the economy it was hurt in  by a
    downturn in tourism caused by the Persian Gulf war and the US recession
