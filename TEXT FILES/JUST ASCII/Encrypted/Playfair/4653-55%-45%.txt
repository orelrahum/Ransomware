     of the population and accounting for  of GDP Industrial activity is

    sugarcane tobacco and grain Production of textiles and carpets has
    expanded recently and accounted for  of foreign exchange earnings in

    growth of  Forty percent or more of the population is undernourished
    partly because of poor distribution Since May  the government has been

    to simplify domestic and foreign investment procedures Economic prospects
