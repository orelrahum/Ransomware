Argentina Economy

Overview
    Argentina is rich in natural resources and has a highly literate population
    an exportoriented agricultural sector and a diversified industrial base
    Nevertheless following decades of mismanagement and statist policies the
    economy has encountered major problems in recent years leading to
    escalating inflation and a recession during  Since  Argentinas
    external debt has nearly doubled to  billion creating severe debt
    servicing difficulties and hurting the countrys creditworthiness with
    international lenders Elected in  President Menem has implemented a
    comprehensive economic restructuring program that shows signs of reversing
    Argentinas economic decline and putting it on a path of stable sustainable
    growth
