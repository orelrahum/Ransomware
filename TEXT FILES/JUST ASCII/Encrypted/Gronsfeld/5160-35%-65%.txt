    which is the main cash crop and major source of export earnings and rice
    Bangladesh is hampered by a relative lack of natural resources population
    growth of more than  a year largescale unemployment and a limited
    infrastructure furthermore it is highly vulnerable to natural disasters
    Despite these constraints real GDP growth averaged about  annually
    during  A strong agricultural performance in FY pushed the growth
    rate up to  and FY saw further though smaller increases in output
    Alleviation of poverty remains the cornerstone of the governments
    development strategy
