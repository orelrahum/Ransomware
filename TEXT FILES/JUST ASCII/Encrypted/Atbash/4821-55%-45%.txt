    problems of economic adjustment  to move away from Moscowbased central
    planning toward a system of decisionmaking by private enterpreneurs local

    requires wholesale changes in supply sources markets property rights and
    monetary arrangements Industry  with  of the labor force  is heavily
    weighted toward the energy sector which produced  of the exUSSRs gas
    and  of its oil Turkmenistan ranked second among the former Soviet
