:Philippines Economy

Overview:
    Following the recession of 198485, the Philippine economy grew on the
    average of 5.0 per year during 198689. It slowed again during the period
    199091. The agricultural sector together with forestry and fishing, plays
    an important role in the economy, employing about 45 of the work force and
    providing almost 30 of GDP. The Philippines is the worlds largest exporter
    of coconuts and coconut products. Manufacturing contributes about 35 of
    GDP. Major industries include food processing, chemicals, and textiles.
